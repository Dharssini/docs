About

Open Function Group#

Open Function Group is a team of ICT4D specialists that have been working exlcusively in data integration, automation and interoperbaility since 2014.

We maintain OpenFn.org, the sector's leading integration platform as a service, and a huge number of open-source integration and "ETL" tools which you can find on our Github.

The platform is trusted by some of the leading development organizations in the world, including UNCIEF, the IRC, and Population Council.
